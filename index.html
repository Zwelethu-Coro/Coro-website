Curriculum

Curro Parkdene School provides a holistic curriculum designed to foster academic excellence and personal growth. We offer the following key subjects:

  • Mathematics – Core and Advanced levels
  • Natural Science – Including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Languages – English Home Language and Afrikaans,and isiZulu 1st Additional Language
  • Social Science – History, Geography
  • CORO(Computers and Robotics) – Coding, Robotics, and Digital Literacy
  • Arts – Visual Arts, Music, and Drama
  • Pitso Mosimane soccer schools(PMSS) – Sports, Fitness, and Health Education

Our curriculum is designed to cater to various learning styles and prepare students for a very successful future.
